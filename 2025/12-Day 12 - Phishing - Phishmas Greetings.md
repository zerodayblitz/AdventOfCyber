# Day 12 - Phishing - Phishmas Greetings
## Overview
Day 12 AoC focused on phishing. Introducing users to phishing, its methodologies, intentions, and sophistication. This task also emphasized the distinction between phishing emails and basic spam.
## Objective and What I Did
Day 12's task required me to triage emails received, separate spam from phishing, and identify the factors related to the email, such as spoofing, impersonation, or social engineering.
## Key Takeaway
Although phishing is one of the oldest tricks in the game, it's still prevalent and only getting more intricate. So, it's important that (not only security professionals) all persons stay up to date on the modern-day factors and tactics used in phishing attempts.
