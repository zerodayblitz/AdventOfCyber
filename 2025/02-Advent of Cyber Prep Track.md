# Challenge 1 — Password Pandemonium
## Overview
To avoid being flagged for a weak password, I created a password that met all requirements:
- 12 characters
- Inclusion of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Not in data breach
## Key Takeaway
Strong passwords are not just about complexity, but also uniqueness and resistance to known compromise.
